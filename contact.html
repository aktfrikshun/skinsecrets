Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and appointments.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling us at (919) 897-0150, emailing us at skinsecretsnc@gmail.com, or using our online booking system. We recommend booking at least a week in advance to ensure availability.

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, we'll conduct a thorough skin analysis and discuss your skincare goals. This helps us create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and concerns.

How long do treatments typically take?

Treatment times vary depending on the service. Basic facials take about 60 minutes, while more comprehensive treatments can take 90-120 minutes. We'll provide you with an accurate time estimate when you book.

Do you offer package deals or memberships?

Yes! We offer various package deals and membership options to help you maintain consistent skincare routines. Contact us for current specials and pricing information.

What products do you use?

We use only high-quality, professional-grade skincare products that are carefully selected for their effectiveness and safety. All products are suitable for sensitive skin and are cruelty-free.
